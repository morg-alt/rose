Seamama Photography specializes in pregnancy, birth, childhood, and wedding imagery has evolved from my own artistic interest in these subjects and my desire to record women’s unique stories and experiences of their pregnancies and births. Pregnancy and birth culturally have been obscured by a veil of secrecy due to society’s taboos around the pregnant body, its intimacy, and power. To bring forth life in all its mystery and wonder is the apex of artistic expression. My vision of this work is to supply women a canvas upon which to express the power, wonder, helplessness, and joy that they may experience on the path to creating life. This is achieved by creative collaborative discussions followed by the creation of intimate environmental portraits, or through my layered land/flower “scapes”, which capture each woman’s unique experiences of body and spirit during pregnancy a time in which they truly in full bloom.

Rosemarie has a B.A. in Photography from Southern Illinois University and a Masters in Fine Arts specializing in Photography from the University of New Orleans. In the early 1990’s she created her business Sea Mama Photography specializing in Pregnancy, Birth and Wedding imagery. She has worked since the 1990’s as a professional photographer and photojournalist: shooting for newspapers, magazines, private companies, and organizations. She has shown her fine art photography in galleries nationally and internationally. Alongside her career as a professional photographer, she has taught fine art photography and photojournalism for over 25 years in secondary schools and Universities.
