Hi! I’m Rosemarie.


So glad you stopped by to get to know a little about me and my work. I've always loved telling stories, but not simply with words. When I found photography, it was the perfect medium for me to artistically express myself. I truly believe that old saying a “picture is worth a thousand words”. With every shoot, I unveil the unique stories of each client and tell them with beautiful narrative imagery. Friends and students have often told me that they feel like they know my clients just from looking at my images. They say photographs are the only tangible memories, which means that the imagery we create together is how you will remember some of the most precious moments. With that in mind, I strive to capture the details (big and small) in the most beautiful light, in symbolic environments/landscapes, and with true emotion and expression. I want you to laugh and cry when you look at your photos. Whether it's your wedding day, yourself pregnant or your babies growing up, time goes by so fast, so it's my job to document your story in all its uniqueness and beauty. My main goal in image-making, whether it be in documenting a woman’s vision of herself pregnant, in capturing a celebration of a wedding or birth, or revealing the tenderness of a lover’s embrace during my travels abroad, is to capture a quintessential moment of intimacy and truth. Whether they are candid moments captured spontaneously or portraits set up consciously and creatively, my desire is to record deeply intimate and precious moments and to create memories with heart and soul that capture the truth of each person’s cherished experiences. I feel honored to give this gift of imagery which allows us to return to these cherished moments, these feelings time and time again.




Biography


Rosemarie has a B.A. in Photography from Southern Illinois University and a Masters in Fine Arts specializing in Photography from the University of New Orleans. In the early 1990’s she created her business Sea Mama Photography specializing in Pregnancy,Birth and Wedding imagery. She has worked since the 1990’s as a professional photographer and photojournalist: shooting for newspapers, magazines, private companies, and organizations. She has shown her fine art photography in galleries nationally and internationally. Alongside her career as a professional photographer she has taught fine art photography and photojournalism for over 25 years in secondary schools and Universities.
